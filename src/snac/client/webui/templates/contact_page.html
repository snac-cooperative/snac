Contact SNAC

SNAC (Social Networks and Archival Context) is a discovery service for persons, families, and corporations found within archival collections at cultural heritage institutions.

If you're looking for information regarding accessing specific archival documents, please contact the holding institution directly.

We value your comments and suggestions. Please use this form to send a message to the SNAC team. If you are reporting a technical problem with the system, please use the feedback option at the bottom-right of the page you encountered the error, if possible, which will provide us with more technical details of your issue.

I understand that SNAC is a research discovery platform for persons, families and corporations hosted by the University of Virginia and the U.S. National Archives. SNAC is not a means of contacting the individual, institution, or holding repository associated with any identity displayed on our website.
